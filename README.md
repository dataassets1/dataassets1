## About Me

I'm a Senior Data Engineer based in Salt Lake City, UT, specializing in **Microsoft Fabric**, **healthcare analytics**, and **cloud-native data platforms**. I hold the **Fellow of the American College of Health Data Management (FACHDM)** designation and bring deep expertise in turning complex health data into actionable intelligence.

Currently, I'm building a cloud-native data warehouse for a nationwide preventive healthcare company — designing scalable, production-grade pipelines and analytics layers on Microsoft Fabric.

I'm also a **Distinguished Top-10 Mentor in Data Engineering** on ADPList and a **Distinguished Jury Member for AI in Healthcare** at the Digital Health Hub Foundation.
